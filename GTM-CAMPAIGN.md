# PaisaWatch — Campaign Strategy & Reel Scripts

**The rule that makes it work:** the villain is never a party or "the government."
It's **the dark you were kept in** — opacity and apathy. That framing is
unifying, undeniable, and it protects the one asset this tool has: credibility.
Stay factual, source every number on screen, and both sides will share it
because neither can call it biased.

---

## Positioning
**"The receipt for your taxes."** A free, source-linked window into where India's
public money went and what the people who spend it have declared. Not opinion.
Not a side. Just the numbers you were owed.

## Emotional spine
- **Vulnerability:** you fund the country and were shown nothing.
- **Lack of knowledge → curiosity:** "Can you even name your MP?" (a dare, not an insult).
- **Empowerment (the flip):** "Now you can see it. In 10 seconds. Free."

## 4 acts
1. **Tease:** unbranded, uncomfortable questions. "Do you know where your tax went?"
2. **Reveal:** "You were never shown. So we built the receipt." Launch.
3. **Empower:** "Look up your own state / MP" challenge → user-generated posts.
4. **Mobilize:** "Tag someone who should see this."

## Content pillars (rotate)
1. Look up your own · 2. By the numbers (one sourced stat) · 3. Your money,
personally (calculators) · 4. How to read it ("pending ≠ convicted").

## KPIs
Plays → **look-ups** (did they search someone?) → shares/forwards → return
visits → subscribers. Constituency-page traffic = "spreading locally."

# Hero Reel scripts (9:16, ~25–30s, captions burned in)

> Music direction is a *mood*, not a specific track — brief your editor to license
> royalty-free audio. Every on-screen number must show its source (e.g. "data.gov.in").

## Reel 1 — "The Receipt" (the anchor film)

Goal: land the gut-punch of never having been shown anything.

| Time | Visual | On-screen text | Voiceover (calm, plain) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0–3s | Extreme close-up of a payslip. A finger slides down and stops on the tax-deducted line. | **You paid this.** | "Every month, before your salary even reaches you — this leaves." |
| 3–7s | The number lifts off the payslip and floats into a plain white space. | | "It becomes roads. Schools. Hospitals. Defence." |
| 7–12s | The screen goes fully black. Silence. Hold the black a beat too long — make it uncomfortable. | **This is everything you were ever shown about where it went.** | (no VO — let the black breathe) |
| 12–17s | A single line of light draws across the black; it becomes the PaisaWatch dashboard filling with real, labelled numbers. | | "You were never given a receipt. So we built one." |
| 17–24s | Quick, clean cuts: a state's spending split · an MP's declared record · "source: data.gov.in" tag visible. | **Real data. Every figure, source-linked.** | "Where the money went. What they declared. In the open. Free." |
| 24–28s | Logo + URL. | **paisawatch.live** · The receipt for your taxes. | "See it for yourself." |

**Caption:** You've paid for this country your whole life. You've never seen the bill. Now you can → paisawatch.live
**Hashtags:** #PaisaWatch #WhereDidItGo #YourMoney

## Reel 2 — "The 2-minute lookup" (the viral engine)

Goal: turn "I don't know" into "wait, *I* can check this?" Highest share rate.

| Time | Visual | On-screen text | Audio |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0–3s | Person to camera, phone in hand. | **"Bet you can't name your own MP."** | VO/creator: "Quick — what's your MP's name? …Exactly. Watch this." |
| 3–8s | Screen-record: open paisawatch.live → tap MPs → type their own constituency. | **Anyone can do this. Free.** | Light, curious beat. |
| 8–16s | The profile loads. Slow-push on the numbers: declared assets, pending cases, attendance %. | **Declared assets · pending cases · attendance — all sourced.** | Creator reacting, genuine surprise. |
| 16–22s | Tap "pending cases" → it opens the actual affidavit record. | **"Pending" = charges, not convictions. Always check the source.** | "It even shows you the original record. This existed the whole time — no one made it easy." |
| 22–28s | Back to face. | **Look up yours. 2 minutes.** paisawatch.live | "Go look up yours. Then tell me you're not curious." |

**Caption:** You can look up your own MP's declared assets, court cases and attendance in 2 minutes. All from official records. Try it → paisawatch.live
**Hashtags:** #KnowYourMP #PaisaWatch

## Reel 3 — "Your salary vs. inflation" (the apolitical on-ramp)

Goal: hook people who "don't care about politics" via their own wallet, then reveal the bigger tool.

| Time | Visual | On-screen text | Audio |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0–4s | Face, blunt. | **"Your salary went up. Did it actually?"** | "You got a raise. But are you actually richer — or just keeping up?" |
| 4–10s | Screen-record: the /tools calculator. Type salary 5 years ago, salary now. | **Free calculator. No sign-up.** | Typing sounds. |
| 10–16s | Result animates: "+19.6% real" or a red "–X%". | **This is your salary *after* inflation.** | "That's the real number. After inflation. Most people never check." |
| 16–22s | Pull back to reveal the full dashboard behind it — tax breakdown, spending. | **Same place shows where your tax goes.** | "And the same tool shows exactly where your taxes go — sourced, free." |
| 22–28s | Logo + URL. | **paisawatch.live/tools** | "Check your money. Then check theirs." |

**Caption:** Is your salary actually beating inflation? Find out in 10 seconds — then see where your tax goes. Free → paisawatch.live/tools
**Hashtags:** #Money #Inflation #PaisaWatch
