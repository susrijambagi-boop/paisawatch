# PaisaWatch — Go-To-Market

The voice: plain, direct, a little angry on the citizen's behalf, but never
shouting. We win with facts, not adjectives. Every claim has a number and a
source. Patriotism here isn't a flag emoji — it's the belief that this country
is worth accounting for, honestly.

---

## The one line
**Your money built this country. You're allowed to see what they did with it.**

## The message (the "why this, why now")

Every month, a piece of your salary is taken before it ever reaches you. You get
no receipt. No statement. No "here's what we did with it." You just trust.

PaisaWatch is that missing receipt. Where public money actually went, what your
MP and MLA declared — their wealth, their pending cases, how often they even
showed up — and whether the money in your own account is keeping up with
inflation. All of it from official records. All of it with the source link sitting
right next to the number.

Not outrage. Not opinion. Just the figures, in the open, for anyone.

Because the moment citizens stop being able to *see* the numbers, the country
quietly becomes a different country than the one we were promised — and we
become people who simply stopped asking. That's the whole reason this exists: so
we don't look away.

## Automating the GTM — honest scope

  auto-draft each week's posts from live site numbers (e.g., "this week's most
  viewed representative", "a state's spending split") so you're never staring at
  a blank page. Say the word and I'll set that up.
- **What needs your accounts (not me):** actually *posting* to X / LinkedIn /
  Instagram. That requires your logins and each platform's API/approval, and
  auto-posting can trip spam rules. The clean path is a scheduler you own
  (Buffer, Publer, or Zapier/Make) — I can pre-load it with the queue above.
- I won't auto-post as you or fake engagement — that would undermine the exact
  credibility the project runs on.
